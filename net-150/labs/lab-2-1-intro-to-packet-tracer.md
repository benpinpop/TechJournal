**Questions**

* How do you deploy workstations and switches in Packet Tracer?
  * We deploy workstations and switches by going to the bottom toolbar and selecting Network Devices > Switches, placing a switch, and using End Devices > End Devices, and placing a computer.
* How do we connect these devices with cables?
  * Go to the Connections tab and select the long black cable. Click on a device and an interface, and connect it to another device with another interface.
* How do you access the command-line terminal on a Packet Tracer PC?
  * Click on a PC > Desktop > Command Prompt.
