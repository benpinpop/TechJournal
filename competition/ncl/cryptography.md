## Fencing

The cipher texts are rail ciphers (transposition cipher), encrypted by spreading a message across rails and reading horizontally to get the ciphertext.

```
W . . . E . . . C . . . R . . . U . . . O . . . 
. E . R . D . S . O . E . E . R . N . T . N . E 
. . A . . . I . . . V . . . D . . . A . . . C . 
```

```
WECRUO ERDSOEERNTNE AIVDAC
```

Source: [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rail\_fence\_cipher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rail_fence_cipher)

Decoder: [https://cryptii.com/pipes/rail-fence-cipher](https://cryptii.com/pipes/rail-fence-cipher)
